§ SECTION 01 — MOVE

Navigate your snake across the hexagonal grid.

W A S D or ↑ ← ↓ → — Change direction

Eat the flashing food cell to grow longer and earn points. The longer you survive, the higher the threat level rises.

Avoid collisions with walls, enemy snakes, and your own body — any contact is fatal.

§ SECTION 02 — COMBO

Eating food in quick succession builds a combo chain:

×1.0 → ×1.5 → ×2.0 → ×2.5 → ... → ×4.0 (max)

Each food eaten within the combo window extends the timer. If you go too long without eating, the combo resets to ×1.

Higher combos mean higher scores — plan your route to chain food pickups efficiently.

§ SECTION 03 — LASER

J — Fire laser beam in your current direction.

The laser has a 5-second cooldown (upgradeable). It damages enemy snakes on contact, removing segments or killing them outright.

Upgrade paths include: multi-beam, pierce (penetrate through enemies), double damage, and slow enchantment.

The cooldown indicator is shown on the HUD bar at the top.

§ SECTION 04 — SKILL

K — Time Slow: briefly freeze the world, then slow enemies and bullets to 20% speed while you move at 70%. Cooldown: 20s.

L — Tracking Missiles: launch homing missiles that orbit your head, then lock onto and pursue the nearest enemy. Cooldown: 10s.

Both skills can be enhanced through the Evolution Tree — increase duration, reduce cooldown, add missile count or pierce.

§ SECTION 05 — EVOLUTION

Collect blue XP orbs dropped by defeated enemies. Fill the XP bar to level up and unlock one evolution node.

Three upgrade paths:

▸ LASER — More beams, pierce, damage, slow enchant. High investment, devastating control.

▸ MISSILE — More missiles, wall pierce, bonus damage. Independent burst path.

▸ MOBILITY — Speed boost, quick turn, time slow upgrades, combo extension, XP boost. Growth-oriented.

Each level-up expands the map, giving you more room but also more enemies.

— FIG. 05 —
§ SECTION 06 — THREAT

Threat level increases over time. Higher threat means:

▸ More enemy snakes spawning on the map

▸ Enemies shoot bullets more frequently

▸ Bullets gain speed and spread patterns at higher levels

Walls appear and disappear periodically — they block movement, lasers, and missiles.

Speed items (▲/▼) spawn on the map — collect them to temporarily boost or reduce your speed.
